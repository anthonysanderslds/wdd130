Whitewater Rafting

At Whitewater Rafting, we navigate the perfect balance between heart-pounding thrills and unwavering safety, creating transformative river experiences that turn first-time paddlers into lifelong adventurers while preserving your safety and the wild waters we cherish.

History

Founded in 1985, Whitewater Rafting began with two rafts and a passion for sharing the thrill of whitewater with others. Based on the legendary Colorado River, founder Anthony Sanders combined his competitive expertise with his intimate knowledge of river ecosystems to create a company that would set new standards in the industry.

Anthony's commitment to safety, excellence, and customer care earned him a reputation as the go-to rafting guide in the region. Whitewater Rafting has became a household name, and rafters from all over the world flocked to experience Anthony's unique blend of thrill and comfort. Come and see for yourself!
